1. Fast and Scalable

Name-driven content delivery paradigm makes it extremely fast and scalable.

ANUBIS

Impenetrable and uninterrupted corporate cybersecurity solution.

ANUBIS is a robust, standalone hardware device designed for corporate networks. It provides impenetrable and uninterrupted protection, allowing businesses to operate seamlessly without disruption. By interfacing between corporate networks and the internet, ANUBIS ensures that malicious attacks are disabled without compromising ongoing IT operations. This rack-mountable device supports and collaborates with existing IT infrastructures, making it a reliable addition to your cybersecurity toolkit.

  • Protects critical IT operations seamlessly
  • Targets financial, healthcare, government sectors
  • Collaborates with existing IT systems effortlessly

BBS

Portable cybersecurity solution for small offices and home users.

The OT-OCN BBS-USB-7 is a portable and convenient device tailored for individuals and small office environments. With its impenetrable and uninterrupted cloud technology, BBS ensures comprehensive protection for all connected devices. This USB-based solution is designed for ease of use while delivering the same robust security benefits seen in larger-scale corporate solutions.

  • Portable USB solution for maximum convenience
  • Ideal for small office and home setups
  • Provides impenetrable and uninterrupted protection
